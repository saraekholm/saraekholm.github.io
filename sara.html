About me

From Fine Arts to Strategy to UX design

Growing up all I wanted was a pen and paper and I was entertained for hours. To study fine arts was a given.

Self portrait from 2007.

Then I started working as a strategist in advertising. It has given me experience in:

     
  • Understanding user needs
  • Qualitative and quantitative research
  • Presentation, workshop and facilitation skills
  • Solving problems in creative teams
  • Handling large and complex clients
I’ve improved the work conditions for nurse’s aids, got more young girls interested in tech, and increased awareness of skin cancer through play.

Seven years and three advertising agencies later it was time for a change. I realised I was in the wrong role - and in the wrong industry.

I’m a Design Lead student at Hyper Island in Stockholm since August 2019. During the same time I’m also studying for an online Professional Diploma in UX Design and a masterclass in Design Sprints.

This has given me the capabilities to work with:

  • Usability Tests
  • Interviews
  • Affinity Diagram
  • Customer Journey
  • Service Blueprint
  • Design Sprint Facilitation
  • Prototyping and Wireframing
  • Structure and Navigation
  • Interactions

In my free time I do improv comedy. It’s theatre without a script where everything is made up on the spot! The principles of improv comedy can be directly transferred into team building and creativity.

I practice, perform and occasionally teach improv comedy. My next shows are in 14 February 2020, you can find the tickets here.
